Technology

When I got interested in gaming youtube I found that recording on my low-powered laptop wasn't something I was capable of. As a result I started researching and digging into the idea of building a gaming computer for cheap. I was in highschool at the time with little funds, so budget was paramount. This early experience with building computers and my love of videography eventually lead me into my love for all things tech. Keyboards, mice, monitors, microphones, cameras, computer hardware, and more!
